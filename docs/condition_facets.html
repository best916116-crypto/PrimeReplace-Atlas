Condition-associated pathogenic-record facets

This page groups public ClinVar pathogenic/likely pathogenic records by ClinVar condition annotation where available, then connects source-locked groups to transcript-unit architecture in the recovered deep-enumerated subset.

Scope. Condition facets are record-level ClinVar annotation groups. They are not disease prevalence, patient cohorts, patient coverage, therapeutic coverage, safety prediction, editing-efficiency prediction, or final implementation-mode recommendations.
