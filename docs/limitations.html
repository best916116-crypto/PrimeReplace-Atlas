Limitations

  • ClinVar record coverage is not patient coverage, prevalence, or allele frequency.
  • The browser does not predict editing efficiency, therapeutic efficacy, or safety.
  • The browser does not provide final PA-family implementation-mode recommendations.
  • Downstream-CDS coverage can be boundary-driven and must be read with payload and transcript-context caveats.
  • Low-record-burden genes are computed but interpretation-limited.
PrimeReplace Atlas Browser release 1.0.0. Record coverage is not patient coverage.
